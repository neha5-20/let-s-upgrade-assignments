LANDOUR

Nestled amidst a canopy of dense Deodar trees, Landour is a quaint town in Uttarakhand

MALLROAD

Located in the heart of Mussoorie, the Mall is a colonial relic with benches and lampposts

Surrounded by high mountain cliffs, Kempty Falls is nestled at altitude of around 4500 ft above the sea level.

Said to be an extinct volcano, the Gun Hill is the second-highest point in Mussoorie
